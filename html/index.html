Cindy Vuong

Bride

Cindy first met Anton at a joint birthday party for Melissa Chan and herself in 2003. There was an instant and undeniable chemistry between the two, as they were both driven by a mutual love of music. They would talk everyday, sharing songs with one another, laughing together and quickly became close friends.

It took two years, before Cindy finally succumbed to Anton's devilish charms, and dashing good looks, an event, that Anton believes to be the best decision of Cindy's life. After driving Cindy crazy for 8 years with his antics, Anton realized that if he wanted it, he had to put a ring on it. And so, on August 29th, 2013, Anton had Cindy picked up in a limousine from work in Downtown Toronto, and sent her on an elaborate scavenger hunt around Mississauga, to recreate the fateful night that Anton asked Cindy to be his girlfriend. His big romantic gesture paid off, as Cindy said yes, and now the rest is history.

Anton Nguyen

Groom

Anton had known Cindy for two years, before he realized how really, really, really, ridiculously happy, she made him, and thus, Anton decided to pursue Cindy's affections. Having been friends for so long, Anton decided a 'slow burn' strategy was best, where Anton would subtly court her, just a little, every day, until Cindy would suddenly realize she had feelings for Anton, but not know how it happened.

After battling the friendzone for seven long months, Anton decided that enough was enough: he would make Cindy his girlfriend! So after a long day, studying at UTM, he took her out for a fancy dinner at Pizza Hut. After graciously paying for the meal, Anton took Cindy home. He was about to ask her out, when something unimaginable happened: He chickened out!

Driving away, Anton began to imagine a life without Cindy, shuddering at the possibility of such a thing. After a boost in confidence from Nia Warrouw, Anton turned the car around, and drove back to Cindy's home. In order to have Cindy meet Anton outside, he shamefully lied to her, telling her that he had lost his debit card on his driveway, and needed her help in finding it once again. Cindy came out, with her flashlight, and Anton unveiled his deceit and revealed the depth of his feelings for her. It worked, and so on November 13th, 2005, they became lovers.
